The Problem

Nigeria has a guidance crisis.

And every year it gets worse.

1.7M
JAMB candidates fail to gain admission every year — most due to poor subject or course selection, not academic ability
13,000
Students per guidance counsellor in Nigerian public secondary schools. The recommended ratio is 1:250
0
Scalable, data-driven student guidance platforms built specifically for the Nigerian education system

Counsellor shortage

Nigeria has fewer than 4,000 professionally trained guidance counsellors for over 13 million secondary school students. Most schools have none at all.

Poor course selection

Over 60% of students choose courses without understanding the career implications. This leads to mass dissatisfaction, dropout, and unemployment after graduation.

Wealth determines destiny

Only students whose parents can afford private consultants or urban private schools receive structured guidance. Rural and public school students are left to guess.

1. What We Collect

ScholarPath collects only the data necessary to generate personalised student guidance. This includes the profile answers students provide (interests, subjects, location, aspirations) and usage data to improve recommendations. We do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what is explicitly provided.

2. How We Use Student Data

Student data is used exclusively to generate pathway recommendations and improvement analytics. Anonymised, aggregated insights may be used to improve our AI models. Individual student data is never sold to third parties or used for advertising.

3. NDPR Compliance

All student data is stored within Nigeria in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR). We maintain a Data Protection Officer and have completed a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) for our core processing activities.

4. Student Rights

Students (and guardians where the student is under 18) have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of their profile and recommendation data at any time through the app or by contacting privacy@scholarpath.ng.

1. Acceptance

By creating a ScholarPath account, you agree to these Terms of Service. Students under 18 must have parental or guardian consent to use the platform.

2. Free and Premium Services

The core pathway generation feature is free for all students. Premium features (detailed JAMB prep schedule, unlimited university comparisons, parent sharing tools) are available by subscription at ₦2,500/month or ₦20,000/year.

3. Accuracy Disclaimer

ScholarPath provides guidance based on data and AI modelling, not professional career counselling. Recommendations should be considered alongside advice from teachers, parents, and qualified counsellors. Cut-off scores and course requirements are updated annually but may not reflect late JAMB or institution changes.

1. Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies only to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. These are strictly necessary to operate the platform and cannot be disabled.

2. Analytics

We use privacy-preserving analytics (Plausible Analytics — no personal data collected, no cross-site tracking) to understand which features students use most. You may opt out at any time in Settings.

3. No Advertising Cookies

ScholarPath does not use advertising cookies or share data with ad networks. We do not track students across other websites.
